The Challenge

What's Holding NCST Back Right Now

NCST Dasmariñas has built a solid academic reputation in Cavite. But every enrollment season, the same gaps cost real applicants — and fixing them doesn't require more staff, just smarter systems.

⚠ Pain Points

💬Prospective students and parents message NCST on Facebook or Instagram — and wait hours or days for a reply. By then, they've already enrolled somewhere else.
📋There is no structured follow-up after an inquiry. Students who expressed interest but didn't immediately enroll are never contacted again — leading to preventable dropout from the funnel.
📅Campus tour bookings are handled manually by phone or walk-in — admissions staff spend hours coordinating visits that could be self-scheduled in seconds.
📱NCST's social media is inconsistent — posts go up sporadically when someone remembers, and the accounts go quiet during semester breaks, exactly when future applicants are deciding where to enroll.
⭐NCST has happy enrolled students, but very few Google reviews — so future applicants searching online see a thin reputation compared to competitors with a strong review presence.

✦ With This System

✅Every Facebook DM, Instagram message, and website inquiry receives an instant, personalised response — 24 hours a day, 7 days a week — so no prospective student ever waits.
✅Every inquirer enters a 7-day email sequence that guides them step by step — requirements, campus tour, deadlines, student stories — nudging them toward enrollment automatically.
✅A self-booking calendar lets prospective students pick their campus visit slot online, any time of day — with an automatic confirmation and two reminder emails sent with no staff involvement.
✅An entire semester of social content is planned and scheduled in one session — posts go out daily across Facebook, Instagram, and Google Business Profile, consistently, without anyone manually posting.
✅Every newly enrolled student receives an automatic review request email — and every positive review is reposted to NCST's social media, compounding the school's online reputation every term.

System 1B

7-Day Enrollment Nurture

Most students need more than one touchpoint before they decide to enroll. This sequence sends the right message at exactly the right time — guiding each inquirer gently from interest to action.

📅

What the Student Experiences

Over seven days, the student receives a series of helpful, encouraging emails — each one adding a reason to choose NCST. They never feel chased, just supported through a decision that matters.

Day 1 — A clear, friendly enrollment checklist: exactly what to bring and how to submit
Day 2 — An invitation to visit campus, with a one-click booking link
Day 4 — Real student stories and scholarship information
Day 6 — A friendly enrollment deadline reminder
Day 7 — A warm final message: "We'd love to have you here"
⏹️

Smart Enough to Stop

The sequence stops automatically the moment a student enrolls — so they never receive a "deadline reminder" email after they've already completed the process. Students who enroll early move straight into the welcome and onboarding track.

No awkward "hurry and enroll" emails sent to students who already have
Enrolled students automatically receive a welcome email and Google review request instead
The admissions team can see exactly where each applicant is in the process at any time
